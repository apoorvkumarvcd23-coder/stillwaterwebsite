Founder & Leadership Team

Amar Kumar Dani

Founder & CEO

Amar has 18+ years of experience in AI across industries. He studied CS from IIT KGP and MSCF from Carnegie Mellon University.

He has started various startups in the past including Shubu.ai, Yota & ShopNTalk which were way ahead of the times.

Now, he is starting Stillwater motivated by his personal experiences of healing himself and becoming disease-free.

Prachi Kumar

CTO

Prachi has 25+ years of experience in Technology leadership roles at IBM, Oracle, EY and now ServiceNow.

She has an entrepreneurial bent of mind and has worked on several startup ideas in the past.

She has joined Stillwater because of her deep conviction in alternative healing techniques.

Nick Nowack

Chief Scientist

Nick is based in the Boston area and holds a BS in Electrical Engineering from MIT, an MS in Cognitive & Neural Systems from Boston University, and trained in numerical methods at the Courant Institute at NYU.

His career spans financial engineering, AI model training, image processing, internet traffic optimization, and signal processing for satellite communications.

As a long-term caregiver for a family member with ME/CFS, Nick has experienced firsthand the power of nutrition and mind-body interventions — including the relaxation response from the Benson-Henry Institute. He has fully embraced signal processing in the body as a path to health.

Captain Joseph Pinto

Chief Healing Officer

Captain Joseph is an ex-airforce pilot and runs organic farming in Mumbai. Like everyone else on the team, he is deeply curious learner and after being a non-vegetarian till 2014, he gave up all animal products and was able to heal himself.

A couple of years back he started the Heal with Pen program in SHARAN through which I got introduced to him and he transformed my life.

Key Advisors

Dr. Nandita Shah

Founder, SHARAN

Dr Nandita Shah, the recipient of the prestigious Nari Shakti Puraskaar in 2016, is the Founder, Director, and trustee of SHARAN.

She founded SHARAN in 2005 with the aim of helping people to heal themselves, and the planet, by connecting to animals and nature.

SHARAN is now a vibrant and growing social enterprise, whose vision is to build a culture of health, and has already positively impacted more than 100,000 lives in India and overseas.
